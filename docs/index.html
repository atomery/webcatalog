Web apps like Google Inbox, Facebook Messenger and Instagram are becoming more and more like desktop applications. But running each of these sites in a separate tab in your browser can be a real pain. It not only reduces your productivity but also invades your privacy with cross-website trackers.

WebCatalog lets you create a desktop app (also called site-specific browser) out of any website, helping you to work more efficiently and protect your privacy.

WebCatalog lets you keep parts of your online life separated into sandboxed site-specific browser that preserve your privacy. Cookies are separated by container, allowing you to use the web without worrying about cross-website tracking tools.

Use your favorite browser as the engine! Or use Juli, an engine optimized for WebCatalog, powered by Electron.
